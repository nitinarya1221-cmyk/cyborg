Computer Vision

Enabling Computers to See and Understand Images

Computer Vision is a field of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to analyze images and videos just like humans. It is used in facial recognition, medical imaging, self-driving cars, security systems, agriculture, manufacturing, and robotics.

About Computer Vision

Computer Vision combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning to process digital images and videos. It allows machines to identify objects, detect faces, read text, recognize gestures, inspect products, and understand visual information automatically.

📷 Image Recognition

Detects and classifies objects, animals, people, and scenes in photographs.

😊 Facial Recognition

Identifies individuals by analyzing facial features for authentication and security.

🚗 Self-Driving Cars

Recognizes traffic signs, pedestrians, lanes, and obstacles for autonomous driving.

🏥 Medical Imaging

Helps doctors detect diseases from X-rays, MRI scans, CT scans, and ultrasound images.

🏭 Industrial Inspection

Automatically detects damaged products during manufacturing.

🛰 Satellite Imaging

Analyzes satellite images for weather forecasting, urban planning, and disaster management.

🛒 Retail Analytics

Tracks customer behavior and inventory using smart cameras.

🤖 Robotics Vision

Allows robots to identify objects, navigate environments, and perform intelligent tasks.
