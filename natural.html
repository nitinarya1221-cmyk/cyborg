Natural Language Processing

Teaching Computers to Understand Human Language

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to understand, interpret, analyze, and generate human language. It powers chatbots, virtual assistants, language translation, voice recognition, and sentiment analysis.

About Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Linguistics to help computers communicate with humans naturally. NLP is used in search engines, chatbots, speech recognition systems, spam detection, automatic translation, text summarization, and intelligent virtual assistants.

💬 Chatbots

AI-powered chatbots answer customer questions and provide 24/7 automated support.

🗣 Speech Recognition

Converts spoken language into text for voice assistants and smart devices.

🌍 Language Translation

Translates text and speech between multiple languages using AI models.

😊 Sentiment Analysis

Detects emotions and opinions from customer reviews, social media posts, and surveys.

📄 Text Summarization

Automatically creates short summaries from lengthy documents while preserving key information.

📧 Spam Detection

Filters unwanted emails and messages using intelligent text classification techniques.

🔎 Search Engines

Improves search accuracy by understanding the meaning and intent behind user queries.

🤖 Virtual Assistants

Power assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant to understand and respond to voice commands.
